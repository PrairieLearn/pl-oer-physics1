A mass $m_1 = {{params.m1}}$ kg is placed upon an inclined plane with an angle $\theta = {{params.theta}} ^o$. The mass $m_1$ is attached by a rope over a frictionless and massless pulley to another mass $m_2 = {{params.m2}}$ kg which hangs vertically. The friction between the mass and the inclined plane is $\mu={{params.mu}}$.


Taking downward as the positive direction for the hanging mass, compute its acceleration. Assume acceleration of gravity equal to $9.8$ m/s$^2$.

What is the tension in the rope?
