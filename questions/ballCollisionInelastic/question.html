An object with mass $m_1 = {{params.m1}}$ kg is traveling with speed $v_1 = {{params.v1}}$ m/s on a frictionless surface until it collides with another stationary object with mass $m_2 = {{params.m2}}$ kg. Assume the collision is perfectly inelastic when responding to each item below.

1) What is the final speed of the object with mass $m_2$?

2) What is the impulse imparted to the object with mass $m_2$?

3) How much mechanical energy was converted into heat in the collision?
