A ballistic pendulum is used to measure the speed of a projectile: a $m = {{params.m}}$ gram bullet is fired into a $M = {{params.M}}$ kg block of wood. The bullet sticks into the wood, and the bullet-block swing up to a height of $h = {{params.h}}$ m. Assume the acceleration of gravity equal to $9.8$ m/s$^2$.


What is the speed of the block + projectile right after the collision?

What is the speed of the projectile just before it hits the block?

Determine the amount of energy lost in the collision.
