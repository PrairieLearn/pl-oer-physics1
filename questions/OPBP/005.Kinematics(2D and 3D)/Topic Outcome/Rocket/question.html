A rocket has a velocity (pointing away from the launch pad) given by $v(t)$={{ params.v_1 }}$t$-$t^2$ where $x$ is in meters, and $t$ is in seconds. Please enter in fractions rather than decimals when applicable (e.g. use 1/2 rather than 0.5)
Part 1

Part 2
(b) What is the acceleration as a function of time in $m/s^2$?
Part 3
(c) What is the average acceleration between $t =$ {{ params.t_1 }}$s$ and $t =$ {{ params.t_2 }}$s$?
Part 4
(d) At what time does the rocket stop rising upwards and begin falling down?
