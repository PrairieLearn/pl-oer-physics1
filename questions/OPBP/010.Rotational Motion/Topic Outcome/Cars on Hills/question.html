Two cars are driving at {{params.v}} $\rm{m/s}$ along the road shown in the figure. Car B is at the bottom of a hill and car C is at the top. Both hills have a {{params.r}} $\rm{m}$ radius of curvature. Suppose both cars suddenly brake hard and start to skid.
Part 1
What is the tangential (parallel to the road) acceleration of car B? Assume $\mu_k = 0.850$ and car B has velocity in the positive direction.
Part 2
What is the tangential (parallel to the road) acceleration of car C? Assume $\mu_k = 0.850$ and car C has velocity in the positive direction.
